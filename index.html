Description of Kitten 1

A kitten is a juvenile cat. After being born, kittens display primary altriciality and are fully dependent on their mothers for survival.

Description of Kitten 2

A feline litter usually consists of two to five kittens.Kittens are typically born after a gestation lasting between 64 and 67 days, with an average length of 66 days.

Description of Kitten 3

The sex of kittens is usually easy to determine at birth. By six to eight weeks this becomes harder because of the growth of fur in the genital region.
